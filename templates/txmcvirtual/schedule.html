TXMC Virtual Tournaments Info & Schedule

Description

We are organizing a series of virtual online competitions for Number Sense, General Math, and Science every Saturday. Both your registration and payment will be done completely online through a secure system. After submitting your payment, you cannot remove your registration. On the day of the tournament (anytime after 7 AM CST), parents (or students) will have to: Print out the test(s) for your students (Tests can be found under the All Tests button on this page), read and sign the honor pledge on the cover page of the test, have students take the timed test, parent or student may grade the test, upload your student's scores before 5 PM CST. After 5 PM, the results/leaderboard will be available. The $5 fee per test also includes an optional training session on the day of the tournament during which we will review some of the problems. The link to the Zoom training session will be provided on the tournaments page.
